MedPfad First Aid Kits

Your offline guide to handling common hiking injuries in Germany. Scan a QR code or tap a kit to view.

📦 Available Kits

  • 🧰 Core Kit
    Included in every kit
  • 🏔️ Zugspitze Kit

  • 🪨 Saxon Switzerland Kit

  • 🏞️ Eibsee Lake Kit

🧠 How to Use

  1. Choose your trail or scan the QR code on your rental kit.
  2. Each page works offline after first load.
  3. Tap an injury to learn which items to use and how to treat it.

🕊️ Safety Reminder

Always let someone know where you're hiking. If in doubt, seek help. This guide is for support, not a replacement for emergency services.
